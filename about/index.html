Main Publications:

Classifying Depressive Severity by Using BCI

Depressive patients present the brain hyperactivity of Beta band in some specific areas under working memory tasks, and these findings can benefit the classifying and scoring of depressive symptoms using BCI. Bipolar disorders show the theta difference in some brain areas under facial emotional Stoop tasks, and these can make the classification accuracy increase.

Psychotherapy Chatbot based on Language Models

A set of therapeutical approaches aimed at fixing practical issues in a client’s life by identifying and overcoming cognitive distortions, replacing maladaptive patterns of thought with healthy alternatives, and preparing coping techniques for physiological and emotional reactions.
By delivering cognitive behavioural therapy text from Psychotherapy Chatbot, users get positive comments and feedback from Chatbot whose professional knowledge support itself to help users. A given therapy usually focuses on one specific problem the client is facing, like treating posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD), depression, bipolar disorder, or aiding in smoking cessation.
